# Nook

Nook is a modern social platform for tracking, collecting, reviewing, and sharing Movies, TV Shows, Anime, Manga, Books, and Videogames in one unified experience.

Instead of separating interests across multiple apps, Nook gives users a personal space to organize everything they love, express their taste, and connect with communities built around shared interests.

## Core Concept

Nook combines:

- Media tracking
- Reviews and ratings
- Social communities
- Personal collections
- Taste-based identity

Users can:

- Track progress across different media
- Rate titles out of 10
- Write and share reviews
- Join communities and discussions
- Create “Nooks” (curated media collections with personal commentary)
- Discover people with similar taste

The platform is designed to feel warm, expressive, cozy, and personal rather than overly technical or corporate.

# Nooks

A “Nook” is a curated collection of media combined with personal expression.

Examples:

- Top 5 comfort movies
- Games that changed my life
- Best romance anime and manga
- Books that feel like autumn

A Nook may contain:

- Multiple media items
- Personal commentary
- Visual layouts/themes
- Reviews and notes

Nooks are designed to be highly shareable and expressive.
